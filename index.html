About

A weekend for the
good ones.

Just a small, invite-only event where we dance and enjoy nature. The plan is to rent a house with a wood floor and a backyard where we can dance, play games, eat some good food, and relax for the weekend.

No set schedule — think of this as a relaxed dance vacation. Feel free to go explore Bangkok throughout the day, but the hope is quality friend and community time in the evenings with dancing sprinkled in.

🤝

Who's Invited?

Invite-only isn't about exclusivity — it's about comfort. Feel free to bring whoever you'd like, but please make sure they're mary jane friendly, non-judgmental, and good vibes only.

🔒

Keep It Between Us

It's important we all keep this a secret for personal, professional, and social reasons. Please take it seriously — don't share with or invite people who would share.

🌿 A note on discretion: The nature of this gathering means we ask everyone to be mindful about who they tell and how they talk about it. This isn't paranoia — it's just good care for the people you love.
